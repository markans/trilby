DKO Architecture

- The Architect

With a 200+ team across six studios in Australia, New Zealand and South-East Asia, DKO is a dynamic design practice whose strenght lies in Innovative design and effective delivery. Opening in 2000, DKO's expertise spans urban design, master planning, architecture and interior design. From bespoke residence to distinctive residential communitites, hotels, unique hospitality, workspace interiors, urban design and large-scale master planning, DKO is interested in exploring the relationshop between people and their environment. At the core of DKO's design philosophy lies a commitment to design that serves people, respect place and stands the test of time.
